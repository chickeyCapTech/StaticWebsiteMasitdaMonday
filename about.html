About Caroline's Concerts

Caroline is a music lover who enjoys attending concerts and discovering new artists.

Out of the five concerts Caroline has been to this year, her favorite was Fontaines D.C., an indie rock band from Dublin. She saw this at The Anthem in D.C.

Her least favorite concert was George Clanton, an indie electroic artist. The music was okay, but the artist drank too much and kept talking about the Red Hot Chili Peppers.
